Gyaan AI

gyaan-ai-beta.vercel.app

An AI learning platform built for students in rural areas who struggle to learn in a language and format that fits them. Gyaan AI covers Physics, Chemistry, Maths, and Biology, and teaches every topic through a structured, multi-step method rather than a single explanation: it first builds foundational understanding — for example, teaching the underlying concepts (like types of triangles) before the subject itself — then walks through the topic in stages, reinforces it with interactive simulations, and finishes with a short three-question quiz. Quiz results are analyzed automatically, and any topic the student hasn't understood is explained again in writing. The whole experience is available in 20+ languages.

Foundation-first, multi-step teaching method Physics · Chemistry · Maths · Biology Interactive simulations Adaptive quiz analysis 20+ languages supported

Indexly

indexly-snowy.vercel.app

A developer-focused indexing tool that solves a real distribution problem: getting a website indexed across every major search engine individually is slow and repetitive. Indexly gets a site indexed across a wide range of search engines — including Google, Bing, Yandex, Naver, and DuckDuckGo — in just two simple steps, with built-in analytics to track indexing performance. It's completely free and open source.

Index in 2 simple steps Covers Google, Bing, Yandex, Naver, DuckDuckGo & more Built-in indexing analytics Free & open source
